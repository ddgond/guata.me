Learning how to find every kabupaten (regency) on the map is the single easiest way to get 5Ks reliably and quickly in Indonesia. Kabupaten are mentioned on signs throughout the country, and finding the right kecamatan within each one is some of the easiest scanning you'll do in your life. Here, I lay out a simple process for learning the location of every kabupaten without breaking your brain in the process. We'll start with memorizing them one region at a time, then move to locating them within a larger region (say, all of Java, or all of Sumatra), then move to locating them in the context of the full Indonesia map. With this process, I estimate it'll take at most 20 minutes per province, totaling (at most) 10 hours of study, though in practice, many of these provinces are small enough to learn in 5 minutes when using my method. Plus, you can always stop partway through, you do *not* need to do this all in one go.
